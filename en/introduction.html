Postgraduate entrance examination story

During my first attempt at the postgraduate entrance examination in late 2022, China had just fully lifted COVID restrictions. I contracted COVID shortly before the exam and did not succeed in being admitted to Sun Yat-sen University.

After that, I spent nearly a year traveling across different parts of China while rethinking my direction and pace of life. In October 2023, I decided to prepare again with a clear target: Software Engineering at Southwest University. I was eventually admitted back to my alma mater with the highest overall score in the program (written exam: 373, interview: 89).
