Research Summary

Research overview

My research focuses on safe control and formal guarantees for stochastic systems, especially reach-avoid controller synthesis, reinforcement-learning-aided verifiable control, and post-verification methods based on stochastic barrier-like certificates, PAC approximation, and sum-of-squares optimization.
