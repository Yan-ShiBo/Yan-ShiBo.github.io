Profile

I am pursuing an M.Eng. in Software Engineering at Southwest University. My current work focuses on reach-avoid control and formal probabilistic guarantees for stochastic systems, connecting controller learning, PAC approximation, stochastic barrier-like certificates, and SOS / SDP solving into an iterative workflow.
